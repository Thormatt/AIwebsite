The 2-Week Sprint

A proven process that delivers clarity without disrupting your operations

Days 1-3

Discovery & Stakeholder Interviews

Understanding your business context, goals, and constraints

  • 90-minute executive kickoff to align on objectives and constraints
  • 4-6 stakeholder interviews across operations, technology, and finance
  • Data and process inventory to capture the current state
Days 4-7

Analysis & Opportunity Mapping

Identifying high-impact AI opportunities specific to your business

  • Process and customer journey mapping workshops (2 sessions)
  • Data readiness scoring across quality, accessibility, and governance
  • Opportunity heatmap evaluating value, effort, and timeline
Days 8-10

Roadmap Development

Creating your prioritized implementation plan

  • Initiative prioritization matrix with value vs. feasibility
  • Investment ranges, resource assumptions, and dependency mapping
  • Risk and compliance review with recommended guardrails
Days 11-14

Documentation & Presentation

Delivering board-ready materials and recommendations

  • Executive playback session with your leadership team (60 minutes)
  • Delivery of briefing deck, investment memo, and pilot charter
  • Leadership Q&A plus 30-day action plan and kickoff checklist
