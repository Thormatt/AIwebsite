Critical Questions for AI-Ready Leaders

These are the questions that separate strategic AI transformation from expensive experimentation.

How can AI create a competitive moat for my business?

This moves beyond cost-cutting to focus on durable, long-term advantage. We'll explore proprietary data plays, unique customer experiences, and operational models that competitors cannot easily replicate.

What "quick wins" can we execute in 90 days?

Building momentum is key. This question targets immediate value creation to fund longer-term initiatives and build internal buy-in across the organization.

Do we have the governance to scale responsibly?

Policy, risk, compliance, and model governance must be in place before pilots become production systems. We identify gaps early to avoid costly retrofitting later.

Where is our data, and is it actually usable?

Data quality, lineage, and access are the #1 blockers. We map your data landscape and identify what's ready for AI and what needs remediation.

How will we measure ROI and benefits realization?

Without clear metrics and ownership, AI projects become science experiments. We define success criteria upfront and build accountability into the roadmap.

What's our path from pilot to production?

Most pilots die in committee. We establish clear acceptance criteria, go/no-go gates, and the operational readiness needed to move winners into production.

The Discovery & Gap Assessment is designed to answer these questions with clarity and conviction.

Guiding Principles

The core beliefs that shape how I work with clients

Strategy First, Technology Second

AI is a business transformation, not an IT project. We start with your strategic goals, not with a list of tools. Technology choices follow from strategy, never the reverse.

Focus on Asymmetric Upside

We identify opportunities where a small, focused AI investment can yield a disproportionately large business impact. The goal is strategic leverage, not just operational efficiency.

From 'Black Box' to Business Tool

My goal is to demystify AI for you and your leadership team, turning it into a predictable tool for growth. Clarity and conviction matter more than technical complexity.

Vendor-Neutral, Always

Unlike large consultancies, I have no implementation teams to sell you. My only incentive is helping you make the right decisions for your business.
