# Interview Guides

## Executive Sponsor (30–45 min)
- Strategic goals and constraints for the next 12 months.
- Top 3 decisions they wish were faster or better informed.
- Risk appetite; what "good" looks like for AI in this org.

## Operations Leader
- Where are the slowest workflows and biggest queues?
- Exceptions and rework—where do they occur and why?
- What KPIs would prove improvement?

## IT / Data
- Data sources of record; quality and access.
- Identity/SSO, RBAC, and environment strategy (SaaS/VPC/on‑prem).
- Observability and cost controls for LLM usage.

## Legal / Compliance / Security
- Applicable regulations; data classifications; DPIA/TRA processes.
- Vendor due diligence; contractual needs; incident response expectations.

## HR / L&D
- AI literacy today; training roadmap; champion network.
- Hiring needs; change management considerations.

## Finance
- Business case standards; ROI threshold; budgeting cadence.
- Cost attribution for compute and data infrastructure.

## Frontline Managers
- Current 'shadow AI' usage; blockers; quality issues.
- Tool switching and manual steps suitable for automation.
