# 30‑60‑90 Adoption Plan (Template)

## Day 0–30 (Stabilize & Learn)
- Baseline metrics; enablement sessions; early champions identified.
- Pilot configured with monitoring and incident playbooks.
- Weekly check‑ins; value & risk dashboards live.

## Day 31–60 (Prove & Prepare)
- Expand to more users; A/B or pre/post measurement.
- Close gaps in governance, data access, and support runbooks.
- Go/no‑go review against success criteria.

## Day 61–90 (Scale & Systematize)
- Roll out to target population; training and comms at scale.
- Cost/latency tuning; production support formalized.
- Value realization review and next‑wave use cases queued.
