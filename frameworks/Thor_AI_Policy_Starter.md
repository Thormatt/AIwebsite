# AI Policy Starter (Internal)

## Acceptable Use
- Approved tools/providers; no personal accounts for work content.
- No uploading of secrets, PII/PHI, or confidential data without redaction and approval.
- Human‑in‑the‑loop for any external or customer‑facing output.

## Safety & Quality
- Prohibited content categories; escalation path for questionable requests.
- Prompt hygiene; retrieval constraints; reference and citation standards.
- Evaluation before launch; continuous monitoring afterward.

## Privacy & Data Handling
- Data classification and minimization; retention periods.
- Tokenization/redaction for prompts and contexts.
- Vendor DPAs, sub‑processors, data residency.

## Security
- Key management, SSO/RBAC, network egress, logging & alerting.
- Prompt‑injection and data‑exfiltration defenses.

## Governance
- Approvals for new use cases; risk assessments; model cards.
- Owner for benefits realization; quarterly value review.
- Shadow AI amnesty + channel for proposing new use cases.
